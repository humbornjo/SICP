Let \phi = (1 + \sqrt{5})/2 and \psi = (1 - \sqrt{5})/2.

When n=0, (\phi^0-\psi^0)/\sqrt{5}=fib(0)=0

When n=1, (\phi^1-\psi^1)/\sqrt{5}=fib(1)=1

Assume fib(n)=(\phi^n-\psi^n)/\sqrt{5}

  fib(n+1)=fib(n)+fib(n-1)
  fib(n+1)=(\phi^n-\psi^n)/\sqrt{5}+(\phi^(n-1)-\psi^(n-1))/\sqrt{5}
          =((\phi^(n-1))*(\phi+1)-(\psi^(n-1))*(\psi+1))/\sqrt{5}

And \phi+1=\phi^2 \psi+1=\psi^2

Thus,

  fib(n+1)=(\phi^(n+1)-\psi^(n+1))/\sqrt{5}

Prove is done.
